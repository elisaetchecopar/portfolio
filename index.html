About me

I’m Elisa, a Buenos Aires-based industrial and UX/UI designer. Currently, I’m involved in research, wireframing, prototyping, and overseeing the design process at my current workplace. Previously, I freelanced for a technology company and co-founded Puie, a cheese brand, where I actively participate in strategic decision-making and various design aspects.

My journey includes a degree in Industrial Design from the University of Buenos Aires, complemented by courses in UX/UI design, advanced UX/UI design, and web development. I have a keen interest in designing both physical and digital products, as well as packaging in my free time. Eager to contribute my skills and continue evolving in the dynamic realm of UX/UI design!
